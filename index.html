School Environment and Student Performance

I analyzed New York City School Survey results from 2018-2019 school year. From a complex set of data that had a lot of collinearity, I discoved that students' perception on safety in and around school is important in predicting students performance in State Assessments.

Combined Disaster Index

As part of a team, we analyzed wildfire, earthquake, and COVID-19 data from 2020 in California. Using cluster analysis, we generated a combined risk index from all three disasters for each county. We also generated maps to easily indetify areas of higher risks due to individual disaster or combined disasters.

Not That Kind of Onion

Built a predictive model for subreddit classification. I collected data using text data through an API and used natural language procesing to remove unwanted text and trained models using meaningful content. I achieved close to 90% accuracy distinguishing a general news feed and The Onion (satirical news media) feed.
